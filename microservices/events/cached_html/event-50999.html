CANCELLED/POSTPONED UNTIL FURTHER NOTICE - DUE TO COVID RESTRICTIONS



RMOTHG HOLIDAY PARTY



December 5th, 2020 11:30 am to 2:30 pm



Its been too long since we have been together. The RMOTHG Social Committee has planned an outside of the box, social distancing, outside, fun Holiday event. It will be Saturday, December 5th, 2020, 11:30 am to 2:30 pm at Bear Creek Lake Park, Pelican Point Shelter, 15600 West Morrison Road, Lakewood, CO 80465 (www.lakewood.org). We will have fun games like cornhole, horseshoes, GPS game, scavenger hunt and you can win a prize! There is a sand volleyball court (bring your ball) available to start up a game, as well as a short walk to trails and accessible fishing pier (if not frozen).



Pink Tank Food Truck is providing our lunch. No side dishes to bring! Just come and have some gastronomical food thats not on your daily meals like Finger Lickin Waffles & Chicken, Youre My Boy, Blue Burger, The Dog About Town Hot Dog (vegetarian option available). Sides of Salted Caramel Sweet Potato Fries or Cheese Fries are available for $3 extra (payable to Pink Tank). Please check out their website at www.pinktank.com.



We will be serving an assortment of Tommy Knockers beer, hot apple cider, hot chocolate, and soft drinks all provided by RMOTHG, as well as assorted cookies for dessert.



The cost for event is $10 per person, non-refundable per CMC refund policy (please see notes). There is nothing to bring but yourself, an appetite, and a smile.



The entry fee per car for Bear Creek Lake Park is $5 for Seniors.



Cash or check donations for the Action Center will be greatly appreciated. No gift exchanges this year.



Please sign-up on the CMC website no later than November 30th.



Any questions, please email Ronnie Knueven at knuevenru@comcast.net.





Please note that we will be following any State of Colorado Covid-19 restrictions.

Notes

Because of cost of $10 for event - non-refundable - Per CMC Policy: "No refunds will be given on or after the sign-up deadline date provided herein. Any requests for refunds received by the CMC Office before the sign-up deadline date will have a $35 processing fee deducted from the original fee or depositi amount refunded. Refunds are issued by checks. Please expect 2 to 4 weeks for payment. The registering member, by submitting the payment required for this event, confirms that they have read and hereby agree to this "Refund Policy."
