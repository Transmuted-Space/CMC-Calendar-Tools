Details

Meet at the trailhead at 11:30am to catch the warmest part of the day. Short hike to Brown Cloud Rocksfor some moderate trad, sport and top rope climbs.https://www.mountainproject.com/area/105744702/brown-cloud-rocks



We'll climb until about 3:30 or 4:00pm.We'll practice some skills and get a few climbs in.



Bring your own harness, helmet, shoes, chalk bag. Club ropes and anchors provided.

Notes

A few things:

  • You can find the latest updates on COVID-19 programming on CMC's websitehttps://www.cmc.org/COVID-19andCMCProgramming.aspx
  • Please review the CMC Protocol Checklisthttps://www.cmc.org/Portals/0/Documents/CMC%20Protocol%20Checklist_1.pdf
  • For more information on trip protocolshttps://www.cmc.org/Portals/0/Documents/COVID-19%20Protocol%20for%20CMC%20Trips_1.pdf
  • All participants sign liability waiver before the trip (VERY IMPORTANT)https://waiver.smartwaiver.com/w/5ad11e69247c3/web/
  • Temperature & symptom check prior to trip. You must report any symptoms and take your temperature 24 hours prior to start of trip. (ALSO VERY IMPORTANT)
  • Face mask worn at all times when encountering other people on the trail, at the trailhead, and when social distance cannot be maintained. I recommend you wear a neck gaiter to easily pull up quickly when you need to. I typically have a face mask for climbing because it stays up better but always wear a neck gaiter. You don't need to wear a face covering while climbing though many of us will feel comfortable keeping it on all of the time and that is perfectly acceptable.
  • Practice hand hygiene. Please bring your own hand sanitizer.
