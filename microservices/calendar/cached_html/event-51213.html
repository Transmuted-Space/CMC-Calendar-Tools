- If you have any symptoms or have been exposed to anyone with symptoms or a positive test, please cancel immediately. If you are currently waiting for COVID test results, you are not able to join us on this trip.

- You must check your temperature prior to the trip. If your temperature is over 100.4 degrees, you must cancel the trip.

- We will be wearing MASK/FACE COVERINGS over nose and mouth anytime you cannot maintain at least a six-foot distance. It will be a casual pace, but if you are too uncomfortable to walk with face coverings, you may need to wait until this CMC restrictions are lifted

- We will be maintaining a MINIMUM 6-FOOT DISTANCE at all times.

- You must have hand sanitizer (70% alcohol) to use.

- You may not share food or water with anyone else on the trip.

- If you develop symptoms within 14 days of the trip, you need to let the trip leader know.

- This trip is LIMITED TO 8 PARTICIPANTS, including the leader.

As CMC Executive Director Keegan Young discussed in his last e-mail to all CMC members, the CMC released trip protocols for outdoor activities beginning on June 1st. The protocols are posted at https://www.cmc.org/COVID-19andCMCProgramming.aspx The page includes information on the CMCs trip protocols, programing, office support, and numerous useful links to other resources on the page.

The CMC will continue to follow the most current Governors Executive Order, which presently mandates a 6 distance between participants and limits each trip group size to 10 members or fewer. There are numerous other requirements for participation in CMC trips. The protocols will be modified based on government directives and other considerations about how we can offer safe activities to all CMC and RMOTHG members. CMC and RMOTHG outdoor trips beginning in June will be posted on the CMC.org calendar and RMOTHG Friday announcements will resume when trips once are offered.
