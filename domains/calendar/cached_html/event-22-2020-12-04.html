Location

The Club is discouraging carpooling, for obvious reasons. However, we can car caravan from the RTD Park 'N Ride, South Santa Fe & Mineral. We will meet in the SW corner of the lot at 7 AM. Look for Steve'sgrey Nissan Xterra.

For those who prefer to drive on their own, the leader will provide directions prior to the hike day.

For more info Contact

Preferred: climbersteveb@gmail.com

Details

Let's enjoy theearly holiday season with a pleasant stroll through Douglas County Open Space. We'll also go through a corner of Roxborough State Park. Have good foot wear, wind gear, and micro-spikes.



Leader wil check the trail a few days before. If the trail is very muddy; that soft, mushy prairie clay; we'll switch the location to Waterton Canyon.
