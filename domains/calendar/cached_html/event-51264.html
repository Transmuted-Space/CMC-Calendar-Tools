Details

25% of this trip is off trail.



If you have any known medical condition that may manifest itself during this activity, please notify the leader via e-mail prior to the trip.



CMC requires we follow all COVID precautions.

Please read this prior to the trip. Details can be found on the CMC web site at this link:

https://www.cmc.org/Portals/0/Documents/COVID-19%20Protocol%20for%20CMC%20Trips%20%281%29.pdf



To recap some of the above listed items:

1. You must have signed a new liability waiver which can be found at this link:

https://waiver.smartwaiver.com/w/5ad11e69247c3/web/

or at the top of the trip sign up calendar page on the CMC web site.

2. Bring hand hygiene and use it, especially at the TH before and after the hike.

3. Masks are required, and need to cover nose and mouth. A buff or neck gaiter is adequate. Masks must be worn at the trailhead, when passing other hikers, and when social distancing greater than 6 feet is not possible.

4. Maintain social distancing of 6 feet. This is the equivalent of an extended arm with a hiking pole.

5. Check your temperature 24 hours before, and again on the morning of. Do not come if you are sick, have symptoms, or have a fever of 100.4 or greater. The nearest hospital is in Jefferson County.

6. Do not share food, water, or first aid supplies. Bring your own wilderness first aid kit.

7. If you develop symptoms within 14 days, let the leader know so that other members can be notified of potential

Notes

The Bobcats mission is to ensure a variety of challenging CMC trips, mostly on weekdays, for our mostly age 50+ constituents. We dont have dues or elections and we invite all CMC members to sign up for any Bobcats trip. We send out a monthly email with all the coming months trips just after those trips are activated. You can search the CMC Calendar for Bobcat trips any time by using an asterisk (*) in the Keyword Search box. To join our free mailing list and become a Bobcats member, write or call CMC Membership Services at office@cmc.org or (303) 279-3080.
