Trip Details

Date Saturday  12/5/2020
End Date
Group Denver
Event Title Avalanche Fundamentals Review
Start Time 7:00 AM
Status Approved
Leader Joe Esseichick
Co-Leader Ken Yaphe
Member Price Free
Available Participants Standby
Waitlist Participants 2
Type Trip
Trip Type Snowshoe
Pace
Classification Easy A
Trail Mileage 4
Elevation Gain 500
Driving Distance 80

Location

Meet at 0700 a.m. at Woolly Mammoth Park and Ride, across from the Exxon station, before leaving for Berthoud Pass/Pump House Basin.

Bring snowshoes, blue books, topo maps w/gradient slope shading, and beacon, shovel and probe!

We'll complete our trip plans in a Zoom meeting, Friday evening, 12/4. Details to follow.

Note: if weather or snowpack conditions are an issue on December 5th, ourback-up date will be December 26th or 27th.

Looking forward to seeing you!

For more info Contact

Ken Yaphe 720-985-9103 kyaphe@comcast.net

Joe Esseichick 734-417-0909 jesseichick@sbcglobal.net

Details

This trip is intended for those who have completed AIARE 1. Objectives will be to review avalanche safety fundamentals to include:



Planning Winter trips into the backcountry



Trailhead beacon check



Proper transceiver use



Recognizing Avalanche Terrain



Relevant observations



Examining the snowpack



Evaluating terrain options



Travel techniques and best practicesto minimize exposure and risk in avalanche terrain



Companion rescue procedure
