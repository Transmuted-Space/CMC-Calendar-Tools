Details

Lets visit Centennial Cone one last time in 2020 before it closes for hunting on Dec. 1 through January. We will hike up the Mayhem Gulch trail, and contnue on to the upper trailhead for a break/lunch. We will return the same way accesing the Juniper trail for a partial loop and then follow Mayhem gulch back to cars. Leader will decide on need for traction devices before hike. We will NOT encounter mtn. bikes as they are only allowed on the trail on even numbered days.
