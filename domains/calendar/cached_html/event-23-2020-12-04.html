Location

This will be a exploratory loop hike in Staunton Park. We will take the Mason Creek trail to the Bear Paw Trail to the Old Mill Trail and back along the Staunton Ranch trail to the parking lot. There will be views (smoke permitting) of Pikes peak from several overlooks on the bear paw trail. This will be 8 miles, with options to extend it to 12 miles if everyone wants to.

Staunton State Park is located about 6 miles west of Conifer. There is a $7 entrance fee per vehicle, unless you have a state park pass. . We will meet at the Home Depot, located on the northeast corner of C-470 and Quincy. Park in the row closest to Quincy. We will depart for Staunton State Park at 7:30.

Here is the address for the Home Depot as people have some trouble finding it. I come from the east on Hampden/285 then get on C-470 to the Quincy exit.

4277 S Eldridge Street, Morrison, CO, 80465
