Trip Details

Date Thursday  12/3/2020
End Date
Group Northern Colorado
Event Title Evening Horsetooth Mountain Hike

Status Complete

Co-Leader Christopher Haugen
Member Price Free

Type Trip
Trip Type Hike
Pace Fast
Classification Moderate B

Driving Distance

Location

We will meet at Horsetooth Mountain parking lot trailhead, ready to hike at 530pm.

Check out Colorado Trail Explorer (COTREX)for trail information :https://trails.colorado.gov/

For more info Contact

To registerfor this trip or if you have any questions, please contact the trip leader Jodi Vanden Eng at warriorjourney1@gmail.com

When emailing (if this is your first time joining) please send me a short description of what recent activity of acomparable fitness level (altitude gain and distance) you have done (in the past month).

Details

This 'trip' is part ofaseries of Thursday after work training hikes/rucks.



We will start at a trailhead off of HT and head to the HT summit and back (via some combination of horsetooth rock, south ridge and Audra Culver trails). We will likely have good views of the stars and planets as well as the city lightsof Fort Collins.



This evening hike will be aweekday workoutfor those of us avoiding the gym due to coronavirus or training for a post covid adventure :) Therefore this 'trip' is meant to be a more rigorous hike and not a "casual jaunt". Round trip time goal is under2 hours(thats a 2.5-3 mph pace uphill-- a fast hike, not a trail run).



Bring the 10 essentials, especially a headlamp andwarmer clothes...as we will be returning in the dark. Be sure to check trail conditions and have microspikes available if needed (currently dry conditions so hopefully not needed).



Individuals without a headlamp and mask (when we meet in the parking area)will NOT be allowed on the hike.





I will send out an update if the trail is closed due to conditions.

Notes

For those wanting an additional fitness challenge, you can bring your own rucksacks, or add weight to yourhiking packs with water or sand bags (recommend starting with no weight if it is your first timeand increment by 5 lbs or 2.5L each time).
