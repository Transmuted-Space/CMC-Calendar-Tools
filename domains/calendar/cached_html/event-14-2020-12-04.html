Details

Colorado is a mecca of outdoor adventure.



Wondering what your next adventure will be? Let us share with you the variety of classes we offer to get you there, regardless of your skill level and experience.



We offer classes for the novice climber up to advanced classes for the experienced alpinist looking to climb more technical routes in RMNP and beyond. From Basic Rock to Technical Ice Leading; multi-pitch rock in Eldo to steep couloir climbs in the Elks; we have a program that will fit your alpine objectives. Our classes are taught by experienced volunteers that want to share their passion and knowledge with you.



Well be discussing different tracks to you can take, including Alpine Climbing School (team track or modular), and our progression through our Rock and Icecourses.



There will be time to answer questions about our schools, prerequisites for classes, gear requirements and any other questions you may have about our Technical Climbing Schools.
