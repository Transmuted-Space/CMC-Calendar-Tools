Details

Join us for our December Trailblazer Crusher Committee meeting! We will be hosting committee meetings monthly to go through action items, leadership, trip creation, and overall revitalization for the Trailblazers Group! If you'd like to get involved, please come out and join in on the conversation of Trailblazers in 2021 at our Crusher Committee Happy Hour!



December's Agenda:




  1. Trailblazer Committee KPIs & Goals

  2. Review 2021 Activities and Activate on Calendar

  3. Leaders for Recurring Local Series

  4. Collaborations with other Special Interest Sections

  5. CATCH UP WITH TRAILBLAZERS!

Notes

We will be meeting virtually via a zoom call. Once things calm down again, we look forward to meeting in-person.
Come drink a beer with us, support your Trailblazer community, and add input if you have a vision for Trailblazers in 2021.
