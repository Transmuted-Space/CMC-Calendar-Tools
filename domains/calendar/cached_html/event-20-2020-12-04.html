Location

Meet at Old Mans Trail, Manitou Springs, CO 80829 (or Memorial Park in Manitou Springs). We will take the shuttle to the trailhead. Alternatively, you can meet us at the incline at 12:30, but let me know so we know to look for you and don't think you just ditched :)

For more info Contact

Audrey Burkart

arburkart10@ole.augie.edu

Details

Don't let the rating fool you; the incline is a tough workout more than an enjoyable hike. Please take a check on your abilities to be sure you're physically and mentally game for this one. Let's go get a workout on the incline! We will gather at the top and eat a late lunch/snack, socialize, then head down together.



We will meet at the free lot and take the shuttle. 12:15 is an odd time, but the shuttle should technically come around 12:20, so please be prompt. Once at the incline, we will travel at our own paces to the top where we will reconvene and walk down Barr trail. It could be icy, so it's always a good idea to have microspikes along. Reservations are required for the incline. You can make one here: https://cityofcoloradosprings.aluvii.com/store/shop/categoryproducts?id=1&offSet=420&categoryId=1



Please reserve a time between 12:30 and 1 pm. Unfortunately, attendance on the trip will depend on if you can get a slot in the reservation; please sign up for the trip when you simultaneously make the slot reservation on the website.



We will follow Covid-19 protocol as noted below:



COVID protocolSUMMARY



1.DO NOT participant on the trip if you feel you have symptoms of the virus



2.Take your temperature 24 hours prior to the trip and confirm to the trip leader it is less than 100.4 F



3.The trip will be limited to 10 total people



4. You must bring a mask to wear at the TH and for when passing other people on the trail.



5. You MUST sign the new liability wavier&l
